Varanam Aayiram

The Romanticisation Of Life We All Deserve

The GVM — Suriya — HJ film is one of the best movies ever to be made in this genre focusing on multiple relationships and their effect on individuals.
The film gives the audience a lot to learn. It stresses the fact that no matter what happens, life has to go on. There are better things waiting for you. கையில் கெடச்சத தொலைஞ்சா இன்னும் ரொம்ப புடிச்சது கிடைக்கும்.
The movie had the right mix of every emotion. Starting from the Father-Son relationship, Husband — Wife relationship and Lovers relationship, the director dealt in such a way that only he can deal with it.
The classy voiceovers, the English accent dialogues, proposals, and scene staging — Agmark GVM.
 The film had some of the classiest proposal scenes ever. I bet everyone would have imagined at least once in our life about Suriya — Meghana Train Journey scenes in our Train Travel (but all I get is senior citizens). Or even the Krishna — Malini proposal scene would come to your mind when you see someone you like.
A father sees himself as a best friend for his son, a mother’s affection towards her family, a man going to any extent for the love of his life (Inga Irukku da America), the pain of the death of a girlfriend (Anjala macha ava), the comeback from it, the long wait for a lady for her love to be accepted, an understanding wife makes this movie what it is.
One of the most pivotal characters of the film appears only for a short period of time but has a huge impact on the lead when he is going through a tough time.
Harris Jayaraj might have provided one of his best albums for this film. Every song is a lovely composition. You cannot choose a particular song as your favorite.
This movie will always be on my list of Top 10 movies which had a profound impact on me. The range of lessons the film gives the audience in each sub-part is immense
Suriya — Krishnan — Malini — Meghna - Priya will forever remain in our hearts.
 varanam Aayiram trulyhit us like a Thunderbolt
